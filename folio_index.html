ABOUT

What you have just seen so far is a body of my recent photographs, exploring the hidden details of New Plymouth, Taranaki.

WHAT WAS YOUR FIRST IMPRESSION?

The photos above are categorised into groups of three. These groups are labelled as greyscale, radiant, minimalistic, striking and elegant. All which are linked to New Plymouth, which is shown through all these images.

The first group to appear that is in fact greyscale. This depicts the darker details of New Plymouth that have been abandoned. Still to this day I find it interesting to capture what is abandoned as we are a society that continues to move foward but aren't active enough to do anything about the materials we already have. Following the greyscale group is the radiant group, which is solely focused on the purpose that at first glance New Plymouth comes off to be a small town. What I have portrayed in the photographs under this category is to have the light breaking through the dull ideas given to New Plymouth; and is almost reaching through the dark alleyway towards the viewer to start seeing this city in a new light.

Instead of explainging all the different groups; it will be interesting to find out whether you agree that the order of the photographs does depict New Plymouth as somewhat cold and abandoned; which is then evolved into something warm and beautiful, once we start seeing more of the city. I look foward to hearing from you!
